### [Slide 5: Feature 1 - High-Concurrency Seat Locking]
**[Visual Idea]**  
A visual of multiple users clicking the same stadium seat. A "lightning bolt" (Redis) grants the lock to the first user and shows a glowing 10:00 timer, while bouncing the others back gracefully.

**[Bullet Points]**  
* **The Race Condition Solved:** thousands of users clicking one seat.  
* **Redis Execution:** `SET(lockKey, userId, { NX: true, EX: 600 })`  
* **Absolute Exclusivity (NX):** Grants the lock exactly once, instantly dropping conflicting requests to maintain fairness.  
* **Time-to-Live (EX):** 10-minute expiry enforces checkout speed, automatically releasing abandoned seats back to the market.

**[Speaker Notes]**  
"What happens when 5,000 fans click the exact same seat at the exact same second? Using Redis, our algorithm executes a `SET NX EX` command. 'Not Exists' ensures absolute mutual exclusion—only the fastest byte gets the seat. 'EX' puts them on a strict 10-minute countdown to pay, or the seat drops back into the pool. No server lag, no double-bookings."

### [Slide 7: Feature 3 - Biometric Anti-Scalping Engine]
**[Visual Idea]**  
A flowchart showing a human face wireframe transitioning into a matrix of numbers `[0.012, -0.45... 128 elements]`, which then locks into a database table.

**[Bullet Points]**  
* **Secure Capture:** React streams webcam feed during cart checkout.  
* **AI Processing Pipeline:**  
  * **YuNet (ONNX):** Precision facial bounding box extraction.  
  * **SFace (Recognition):** Transforms the face into a 128-dimensional topological string.  
* **Data Security:** We do not store raw photos—we store mathematical arrays. Safe, GDPR-compliant, and impossible to reverse-engineer.

**[Speaker Notes]**  
"This is our crown jewel. During checkout, we don't ask for a captcha; we ask for your face. Our Python microservice uses YuNet to find the face and SFace to compress it into a 128-dimensional mathematical vector. We store the math, not the photo. If a scalper tries to sell the ticket, the ticket is useless, because they cannot sell their face with it."
